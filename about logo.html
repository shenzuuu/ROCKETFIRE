ABOUT LOGO


This logo was developed to represent the application itself, which focuses on providing users with measuring services. When a user first encounters our application, we want to convey to them the objective of it using the visual of a tape measuring a mannequin of the body. In terms of the colour chosen for this logo, we went for grey. The significance of this shade is intelligence and compromise, and we want to create a service that combines the best technology, intelligence, and user satisfaction. It also contributes to the application's first impression of being classy and cool. As for the colour white, it stands for positivism, truth, and honesty. It also stands for everything morally upright and good. Our trademark or tagline, "meet your desire," is also included in this logo. We want the user using our application to be pleased with their decision of size and measurement.
